MSC Sinfonia


Company:MSC Cruises


MSC Sinfonia is a cruise ship owned and operated by the Italy-based MSC Cruises. She was built in 2002 by Chantiers de l'Atlantique in Saint-Nazaire, France for the now-defunct Festival Cruises as MS European Stars.


A cruise ship of the Lirica class. Owned and operated by MSC Cruises. The vessel is designed for 2,546 passengers and 721 crew members.


MSC Lirica


Company:MSC Cruises


MSC Lirica is the lead ship in her class of cruise ship, owned and operated by MSC Cruises. She was the first newbuild cruise ship to enter service for MSC Cruises. She can accommodate 1,560 passengers in 780 cabins.


MSC Lirica brings you a distinctive cruise experience centred on elegance, comfort and hospitality in the finest MSC Cruises tradition.
